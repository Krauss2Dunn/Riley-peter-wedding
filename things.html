Things To Do

Make a weekend of it.

From waterfalls and wineries to riverfront strolls and small-town exploring, there is plenty to enjoy in and around the Gorge.

Scenic Drive

The Hood River Fruit Loop

A 35-mile scenic drive through orchards, vineyards, farm stands, bakeries, and wineries around Mount Hood. It’s one of the best ways to experience the region and a perfect weekend adventure.

Outdoors

Waterfalls & Hiking

The Columbia River Gorge is famous for its waterfalls and dramatic trails. If you have time, explore nearby hikes and scenic viewpoints throughout the Gorge.

Food & Drink

Wineries & Breweries

Hood River and the surrounding valley are full of excellent wineries, cideries, and breweries with beautiful views.

Town

Explore Hood River

Spend an afternoon wandering Hood River’s shops, cafés, riverfront parks, and restaurants overlooking the Gorge.
