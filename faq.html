FAQ

A few things to know.

What should I wear?

Welcome Party: Elevated Casual — look good and bring a second layer if it gets chilly in the evening.

Wedding: Colorful Formal — long dresses, suit and tie.

Sunday Sendoff: Hangover Chic — whatever you’re capable of wearing.

What about transportation?

Please see the Travel page for the basics. We strongly encourage guests to rent a car in Portland.

Taxis and ride-shares are not available in the wedding area.

Please do not drive to the venue. Shuttles will run from the main hotels to the venue, so if you’re staying elsewhere, drive to one of the hotels and hop on the shuttle.

What will the weather be like?

Please pray to your deity of choice for sun.

Late May tends to be mild, with daytime highs around 70 and nighttime lows around 50.

It’s the Pacific Northwest, so rain is always possible.

Are children invited?

No.

What kind of shoes should I wear?

The welcome party and ceremony will likely take place in a grassy field, so please choose shoes that can handle uneven outdoor terrain.

What if I have another question?

Just ask us.
