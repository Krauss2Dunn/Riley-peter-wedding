Travel & Lodging

The Columbia River Gorge

Wind Mountain Ranch sits above the Columbia River Gorge in Stevenson, Washington — about an hour from Portland. The area is known for waterfalls, orchard, vineyards, hiking, and sweeping views of the river valley.

We recommend flying into Portland International Airport and renting a car. It is a stunning, one hour drive from Portland to the Hood River area.

Taxis and Ubers are not common in the Hood River area, so having a car will give you flexibility during the weekend.

We will have shuttles to and from the wedding location and hotels.

Airport

The closest major airport is Portland International Airport (PDX), approximately one hour from Stevenson.

Stevenson

Stevenson sits on the Washington side of the Gorge and offers beautiful river views and quiet mountain surroundings.

Hood River

Just across the river, Hood River offers restaurants, breweries, and additional lodging options.

Things To Do

Make a weekend of it.

From waterfalls and wineries to riverfront strolls and small-town exploring, there is plenty to enjoy in and around the Gorge.

Scenic Drive

The Hood River Fruit Loop

A 35-mile scenic drive through orchards, vineyards, farm stands, bakeries, and wineries around Mount Hood. It’s one of the best ways to experience the region and a perfect weekend adventure.
